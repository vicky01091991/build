About BuildMyNest

Your Trusted Partner in Construction, Renovation & Restoration

BuildMyNest Trusted Construction Experts

Building Dream Homes with Quality, Precision & Trust in Bangalore

With over 21 years of hands-on experience, Vijay, our Founder & Managing Director, has shaped Bangalore’s residential landscape through standalone buildings and high-rise apartments, all built with uncompromising quality and long-term value.

BuildMyNest specializes in turnkey residential construction, renovation, restoration, structural repair, and premium remodeling solutions. From design planning to final handover, we deliver transparent pricing, branded materials, and unmatched craftsmanship — ensuring your dream home is built to last.

Guided by the motto, “Building Dreams with Unwavering Quality and Trust,” Vijay focuses on sustainable growth, green building practices, and creating homes that families can truly rely on.
